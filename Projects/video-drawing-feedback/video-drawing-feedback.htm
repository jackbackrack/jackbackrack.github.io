Video Drawing Feedback
What A new tool for guiding human drawing with video feedback
Which We're twitchy machines that make quaint but comforting mistakes and the computer is using the human to glitch drawing by hand with graphite powder and pencil
Why When drawing by hand and with my eye, I always was dissatisfied with the process. I'm feeling that there are things that computers are better at and things that humans are better at. This is an attempt to get the best of both worlds, to speed up drawing, and to allow a larger range of creative possibilities.
