art maintenanceAt a reasonable volume, the device should run for 8 hours or more at a stretch (though the LED peak tracking mode only operates at higher volumes). However as a precaution it should be kept out of overly warm locations to prevent overheating. The system is battery operated and therefore needs to be recharged each day. The two batteries must be recharged SEPARATELY or a terminal short condition will occur; unless two separate rechargers are used that have *distinct, electrically isolated grounds*. Charging the batteries separately is the safest method of operation.
art autoSimply turning on/off power to the backpack and also to the audio input device.
art soundThe backpack simply amplifies the audio input signal with a constant gain; volume can be set to any desired level on the input device.
art lightThe device has two LED modes - constant, bright illumination (24 bright red LEDs) or audio peak tracking (LED brightness fluctuating with input signal at louder volumes). It looks better in a somewhat dim environment.
art needsThe piece would look best mounted on the back of a mannequin, as it is intended to be worn by a person. Otherwise, placed on a stand with appropriate protection from falling would be OK. It requires a suitable portable audio input device (iPod or similar). In a gallery setting, audio input from a hidden external source seems like it would be OK.
